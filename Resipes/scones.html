Scones

Soft, light and easy scones recipe. These treats are always welcome among guests .Offer a variety by serving half the scones with a savoury topping such as butter and cheese and the other half with jam and cream. What you'll need

  • 3 eggs
  • 180ml milk
  • 6 cups plain flour
  • 0.75 tsp salt
  • 12 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup cooking oil

Preparation

  1. Mix eggs, milk and oil in a bowl.
  2. Sift in the dry ingredients.
  3. Knead lightly on a floured surface.
  4. press down to approximately 1cm thick.

Bake it!

  1. Cut round scones with a cookie cutter.
  2. Place on a greased baking tray.
  3. Eggwash or paint with milk.
  4. bake for 15 - 20 minutes.
