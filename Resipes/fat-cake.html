Fat Cakes

Famously known as amagwinya, a delightful kasi snack commonly enjoyed with cheese, polony, mince etc..

What you'll need:

  • 7 cups of all purpose flour
  • Half a cup of sugar
  • 10g of yeast (1 sachet = 10g)
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 2 and half cups of warm water
  • Oil

Preparation

  1. Place all your dry ingredients (flour, sugar, yeast and salt.) in your mixing bowl and mix together well.
  2. Add water and 2 tablespoon of oil and mix well. You may use a mixer but if you do not have one you can use your hands. After mixing your dough should be sticky.
  3. Cover your dough with a cloth and let it rise for 45 minutes. (place in direct sunlight for best results)
  4. Your dough should be twice the size. Knead your dough one more time and let it rest for another 15 minutes.

Let's get cooking!

  1. Heat up your oil at a medium temperature. Make sure you put enough oil for deep frying
  2. Separate your dough into balls (or any shape you desire) and fry until golden brown (approximately 10 minutes).
